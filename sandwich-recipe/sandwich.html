Sandwich

Hi! Today we will be making a sandwich!

Ingredients

We will need the following ingredients:

  • Sandwich Bread
  • Sliced Meat
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Sliced Lettuce

Steps

Here are the following steps:

  1. First, Place down two slices of sandwich bread on a plate
  2. Place one slice of meat on each piece of sandwich bread
  3. Now place one piece of cheese on each piece of sandwich bread
  4. Finally, put a piece of lettuce on each piece of sandwich bread
