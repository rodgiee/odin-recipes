Sunny-Side Up Eggs

Hi! Today we will be making Sunny-Side Up Eggs!

Ingredients

We will need the following ingredients:

  • Eggs (of course!)
  • Cooking Spray
  • Salt(optional)

Steps

Here are the following steps:

  1. Preheat the pan
  2. Add cooking spray to the pan
  3. Crack desired amount of eggs into the pan
  4. Let eggs cook for how cooked you want!
